Local anesthesia




Local anesthesia is a virtual reality training simulator for performing local anesthestic prodecures. The app is the demo of local anasthesia in a dental setting. The injection is connected to a haptic stylus (Phatom Touch X ) which provide vibrotactile feedback whenever it touches the surface of the virtual object. So , a little bit of force is required to pierce the injection through the skin like in real life to deliver the chemicals for the prodecures. This simulator is designed to train medical students about what to do and what not do during the procedures. A slight mistake can be lethal to the patient so this simulator properly trains the students before they do their procedures in real life.
